Just Starting Out?

Trying new art styles is always a little difficult when you aren't exactly sure what you're getting yourself into. View our "graffiti basics recommedation" list to start a kit that wont break the bank.

  • Blank Stickers
  • 2 colors of your choice spray paint
  • Highlight medium of choice
Details
  1. If you're nervous starting out, try drawing pieces on stickers and putting them around your city.
  2. My recommendaation is to start with black and white then begin venturing out.
  3. To add highlights to your piece try using mop pens or paint maarkers.

Good luck on your first piece and dont forget to tag us @OzzieVerse on all socials!
